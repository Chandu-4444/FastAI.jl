FluxTraining.step!

step!(learner, phase::Phase, batch)

Run one step of training for learner on batch. Behavior is customized through phase.

Extending

This is a required method for custom FluxTraining.Phases.Phases to implement. To implement step!, it is recommended you make use of FluxTraining.runstep to get begin and end events as well as proper handling of CancelStepExceptions.

See the implementations of FluxTraining.Phases.TrainingPhase and FluxTraining.Phases.ValidationPhase for reference.
